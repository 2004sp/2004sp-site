# World Map

The Lost City server runs the authentic 2004 RuneScape map — every location, dungeon, and region from that era of the game is present and accessible.

## Key Locations and Bot Activity

**Lumbridge** — Spawn point. Bots begin here and immediately start training the most appropriate skill for their current level. Lower-level bots fish in the river or cut trees near the cow field. Mid-level bots progress toward Draynor or Varrock.

**Draynor Village** — A hub of activity. Willows are cut here constantly. Thieving bots work the stalls. The bank sees heavy traffic throughout the day.

**Varrock** — The commercial centre. Varrock West Bank is the primary banking point for most smithing and crafting activities. The Grand Exchange area (as it existed in 2004 — before the Exchange itself was added) sees bot trading.

**Falador** — Mining Guild access for coal and mithril. Bots begin migrating here once their Mining level reaches the 60s.

**Al Kharid** — The toll gate is handled correctly. Bots pay the 10-coin toll or use the Lumbridge/Shantay Pass route. The duel arena is active for PvP events.

**Barbarian Village** — Fishing with harpoons and lobster pots. Agility training on the Barbarian Outpost course at higher levels.

**Edgeville and the Wilderness** — Runecrafters traverse the Abyss. PvP bots patrol the Wilderness levels appropriate to their combat. The Chaos Altar and King Black Dragon lair are navigable.

**Karamja** — Boat navigation is implemented. Bots sailing to fish for lobsters and tuna at the Karamja docks is one of the most visually satisfying bot behaviours to watch.

## Unexplored Territory

Not every area of the 2004 map has full bot coverage. Members areas receive more bot activity when the Extras content is installed. Contributions covering Morytania, Kandarin, and the Fremennik Provinces are welcome via the Extras repository.
