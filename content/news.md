# Latest News and Updates

## 2004SP Project News

**Lost City Singleplayer Progressive** is live. Extract, run `start.bat`, login, and watch AI bots populate the world in real time.

Autonomous bots now progress across all 19 skills — Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, Range, Prayer, Hitpoints, Fishing, Woodcutting, Mining, Runecrafting, Firemaking, Fletching, Smithing, Cooking, Crafting, Herblore, Agility, and Thieving.

Bots trade with players, fight in the Wilderness, complete quests, open gates, navigate Al Kharid tolls, and sail to Karamja — all without any player input.
