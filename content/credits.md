## Historical Acknowledgement — RS2 Progressive by mige

2004SP Progressive is a spiritual successor to **EasyRS2 / RS2 Progressive**, originally created by **mige**. That project was one of the earliest attempts at a progressive single-player RS2 experience. It proved the concept worked — that a single-player RuneScape world populated by progressing bots was not only possible but genuinely compelling.

The launcher and client modification from that era are preserved as historical artefacts. Both mirror links are maintained so the original work stays accessible:

- [Archive.org mirror](https://archive.org/details/rs2-prog-4.5-mige-complete-launcher)
- [MEGA mirror](https://mega.nz/file/24422AoJ#ophyBGr02tjkKaNwzxVI7GZJ0h9AZj8amC5f7-q__W4)
