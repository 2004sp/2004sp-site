# Create an Account

Lost City Singleplayer Progressive uses the original Lost City 2004 account system. Accounts are local — stored on your machine, requiring no registration with any external service.

## Setting Up Your Character

When the server starts for the first time, you can create an account directly from the game client login screen. Choose any username and password. Your character begins at Lumbridge, level 3, with starter equipment — exactly as the original 2004 experience dictated.

## What to Expect

Within minutes of logging in, you will see AI bots walking through Lumbridge, chopping trees outside Draynor, fishing on the Barbarian Village docks, and heading to the Wilderness for PvP.

Bots are aware of other players. They will interact with you, offer trades, and occasionally attempt to scam you — faithful to the social dynamics of 2004 Runescape.

## Multiple Accounts

The server supports multiple simultaneous accounts. You can run a second client instance to play as a second character or observe the world from a different vantage point.

## Joining the Community

A Discord server is available for help with setup, sharing screenshots, reporting bugs, and discussing bot behaviour and world events. The community is small but active — join via the Discord button on the main page.

All progress is saved locally. Your character's stats, items, and quest completions persist between sessions.
