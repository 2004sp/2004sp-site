# Screenshots

A selection of images from Lost City Singleplayer Progressive and the original 2004 RuneScape client.

## The Original Homepage — August 2004

The runescape.com homepage as it appeared in December 2004. This project faithfully reconstructs that era of the game engine.

## Game Select Screen

The character and world selection interface from the 2004 client. Lost City Singleplayer Progressive uses this original client with optional extended-feature modifications available via the Client repository.

## Dynamic Hiscores

The hiscores table updating in real time as bots level skills. Your character competes on the same leaderboard — watch your rank shift as the bots progress overnight.

## Full Hiscores View

The complete hiscores listing across all skills. Bots are indistinguishable from player characters on the leaderboard, creating the authentic atmosphere of a populated 2004 server.

## Contributing Screenshots

Have a great screenshot from your session? Share it in the Discord and the best ones will be added to this page. Particularly sought after: Wilderness PvP between bots and players, rare drop screenshots, and bot behaviour you did not expect.
